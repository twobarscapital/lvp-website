About Lobito Venture Partners

Lobito Venture Partners is a mission-driven development finance vehicle deploying capital directly into real assets across the Lobito Corridor. Every asset in the portfolio is intended to become a permanent local asset — owned, operated, and maintained in-country — creating lasting infrastructure, employment, and economic capacity for African communities. LVP bridges the gap between Western capital and African development without the overhead of traditional DFI channels.

Geography

The Lobito Corridor

A transformative rail, road, and logistics backbone linking the mineral heartlands of the DRC and Zambia to the Atlantic port of Lobito.
